About Dystopian Times

Based on a concept by Maciej Kuciara for the Last of Us series.

In order to get consistent blending between the rubble and the ground I used a world mapped ground texture as base for the rubble object. This ensured that the unique parts of the model wouldn't be ruined by unrealistic blending or ugly seams.
